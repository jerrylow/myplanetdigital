Myplanet Wins Markham Board of Trade 2012 Business Excellence Award

We're pleased as all-get-out to be the recipients of the Markham Board of Trade 2012 Business Excellence Award for Entrepreneurship. Since our humble beginnings in 2009, we've bootstrapped our way to nearly 50 brilliant team members and a great client list. And although we still have a long way to go in our quest to become a world-renowned user experience company, it's great to be recognized for the huge gains we've made.

Here's to a monumental 2013!
